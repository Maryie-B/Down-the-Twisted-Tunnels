Down the Tistwed Tunnels

You find yourself walking down a long, poorly lit hallway. The cold and dry air presses on the hard stone walls and something in your gut hints to you that you are now lost hundreds of meters underground in a strangely ancient construction where for a long time only dust and echoes have roamed about. Try as you might, you find no memory of how you got there and no clue on how to find your way out…
You can make your choices pressing the buttons. In order to complete the game you need to find your way out of the twisted tunnels. You can take the same route multiple times, turn back and try again. Remember your way, lest you get even deeper into the chaotic maze of stone, and don’t let your sanity slip away while enjoying the madness…

You open your eyes, feeling a sense of relief quickly spreading within. The tunnels are gone and everything around you looks normal again. Looking at the screen and reading these words you know you are in the real world once more and you can’t help but enjoy the freedom, knowing you escaped those tunnels and you are, even if just a little bit, stronger.
